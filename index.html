Stakify Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

This policy explains what data Stakify ("the app", "we") collects, why, and how it's handled.

Who we are

Sandlab, contactable at sandlab.apps@protonmail.com.

Account

Stakify creates an anonymous account for you on first launch — there's no sign-up, no email or password. Your account is tied to your device installation via a random ID. If you delete and reinstall the app, a new anonymous account is created and your previous data is not carried over unless you contact us.

What we collect

  • App usage you choose to lock: which apps you select to gate (via Apple's Screen Time / Family Controls), and how many minutes you spend in them per day. We only see aggregate minutes-used, never what you did inside those apps, your browsing history, or message content.
  • Game and wallet activity: your minute balance, daily budget, game rounds played (game type, stake, result), win/loss totals, streak, and level/XP.
  • Profile info you provide: display name, age range, and goal, all optional and self-reported during setup.
  • Friends: if you add a friend by code, we share your level/XP and daily screen-time/game activity with that friend (and theirs with you) — never your wallet balance or display name beyond what you've set.
  • Phone number (optional): if you enable contact-based friend discovery, your phone number is hashed on your device before it's sent to us — we only ever store that hash, never your raw number. Contacts you sync are hashed locally too; only the hashes are checked against our database, and non-matching hashes are discarded immediately, never stored.
  • Purchases: subscription purchases are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We receive purchase/entitlement status (active or not) but never your payment details — those stay with Apple.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't run ads or third-party ad-tracking SDKs in this app.
  • We don't read the content of the apps you lock — only the fact that you used them and for how long.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact sandlab.apps@protonmail.com with your anonymous user ID.

Age requirement

Stakify includes simulated-gambling-style game mechanics (using in-app minutes, never real money). It's intended for users 17 and older and is not directed at children. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We'll update the "last updated" date above if this policy changes materially.

Contact

Questions about this policy: sandlab.apps@protonmail.com.
